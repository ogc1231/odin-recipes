Pasta

Ingredients

  • Semolina Flour
  • Eggs
  • Water
  • Salt

Steps

  1. One whole large egg per 100g of flour
  2. Add a pinch salt
  3. Add a bit of water if too dry
  4. Knead lightly
  5. Wrap in film and rest in fridge for 1-2 hours
  6. Roll out dough in your prefered shape
  7. Cook in salted boiling water
  8. Drain water and serve as soon as possible
